The bulk of modern work is more team based than ever.  People are incouraged to work together because studies have shown  that working in teams achieve better results.  
Project Aristotle reviewed half a century of academic studies looking at how teams work.  It scrutinized how teammates socialize outside of work  and if they had the same hobbies.  Basically, was a team that was connected outside of work more effective than a team that only connected at the workplace.  Project Aristotle researchers concluded that understanding and influencing group norms were the keys to improving teams. There were  behaviors that seemed important, like making sure teams had clear goals and creating a culture of dependability.
